Liz's Familia & Amigos Circle

The Idea

This website to help plan and execute social gathering with immediate family and friends to strengthen relationships. This will focus on creating organized activities like monthly dinners, weekend hangouts, and even game nights. Family and friends can input activity preferences before each event via chat. This is to keep in touch with with each other between our busy lives.

I hope everyone that is willing to spend a little time getting to use this site will see the big picture and participate in the events planned within our family and friends.
